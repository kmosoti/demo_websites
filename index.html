About Us

Judith Nyairo is a dedicated psychiatrist with extensive experience in mental health. She specializes in providing personalized care to her patients, focusing on holistic treatment approaches. Dr. Nyairo is committed to creating a safe and welcoming environment for all her patients.

  • My Mission

    My mission is to offer empathetic, patient-centered psychiatric care to enhance the mental well-being of our community.

  • My Vision

    To be a leading psychiatric practice known for innovative and compassionate care in Arlington.
